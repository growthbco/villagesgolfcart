Daily Rental FAQs

What's the minimum rental period?

We require a 3-day minimum for all daily rentals. This ensures fair pricing and cart availability for all customers. Perfect for weekend getaways!

Can I get same-day delivery?

Yes! Orders placed before 2 PM can be delivered the same day within a 2-hour window. Perfect for emergencies or last-minute plans. Call 352-555-0123 for urgent requests.

What's included in daily rental rates?

Every daily rental includes free delivery and pickup anywhere in The Villages, full tank of gas, $20 fuel surcharge, all safety equipment, and applicable taxes. No hidden fees!

Can I extend my rental if needed?

Absolutely! Additional days are available at competitive rates. Just call us and we'll extend your rental based on availability.
