Questions? We've Got Answers! 🤔

Here's what our neighbors usually ask us

How much does golf cart rental cost in The Villages?

Our transparent pricing starts at $209.63 for a 3-day minimum rental of our cozy 2-passenger cart, $231.13 for our popular 4-passenger cart, and $263.38 for our spacious 6-passenger cart. All rates include a $20 fuel surcharge and applicable taxes. After the minimum, daily rates are just $20/day for 2-passenger, $25/day for 4-passenger, and $35/day for 6-passenger plus tax. Weekly and monthly rentals offer even better value! No hidden fees, ever!

Do you deliver golf carts to all Villages locations?

Absolutely! We provide FREE delivery and pickup to every single Villages location including Spanish Springs, Lake Sumter Landing, Brownwood, Sawgrass Grove, Fenney, Rohan, Water Lily, Eastport, and everywhere in between. We know these streets like the back of our hand!

Can I book a golf cart rental online?

You sure can! Book online 24/7 right here on our website, give us a call at 352-306-4409 (we love chatting!), or use our live chat feature. We're here to help however you prefer!

What's included with my golf cart rental?

Everything you need! Every rental includes free delivery and pickup anywhere in The Villages, a full tank of gas, all safety equipment, 24-hour roadside assistance through our partnership with 24hrcartclub.com, and we even throw in a complimentary Villages area map for newcomers. Peace of mind is included!

Are your golf carts safe and reliable?

Safety first! Every cart is inspected, cleaned, and maintained to the highest standards. All our carts have seatbelts, lights, turn signals, and are Villages community approved. We treat every cart like it's going to our own family!

How long have you been serving The Villages?

We've been proud Villages business owners since 1999! We've watched this amazing community grow and have been honored to serve thousands of wonderful neighbors over the years. We're committed to continuing our tradition of excellence! 💚
