Daily Rental FAQ

Common questions about our daily golf cart rentals

Can I rent a golf cart for just one day?

While our standard minimum is 3 days, daily rates are available after meeting the minimum. For special circumstances requiring a single day rental, please call us at 352-306-4409.

Do you offer same-day rentals?

Yes! We offer same-day availability when carts are available. Call us early for the best chance. During peak season, we recommend booking in advance.

Can I extend my rental by the day?

Absolutely! If you already have a 3-day or weekly rental, you can extend it at the daily rate. Just call us before your rental ends.

Is delivery included?

Yes, free delivery and pickup is included with all rentals to any location within The Villages, Lady Lake, and Fruitland Park areas.
