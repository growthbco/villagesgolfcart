Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (November-April). However, we often have last-minute availability!

What if I need to cancel or change my reservation?

No problem! We offer flexible cancellation up to 24 hours before your rental. Changes can be made anytime - just give us a call!

Do you offer emergency roadside assistance?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency support. If you have any issues with your cart, just call our emergency line and we'll help immediately.

Can I extend my rental while I have the cart?

Absolutely! Just call us and we can extend your rental. We'll adjust the pricing accordingly and you can keep enjoying your cart!
