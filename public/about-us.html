How It All Began

From humble beginnings to serving thousands of happy customers

2010 - The Beginning

Our founders moved to The Villages and noticed their neighbors needed reliable golf cart rentals. With just 3 carts and a dream, our business was born.

2015 - Growing Together

Our fleet grew to 15 carts, and we became the go-to choice for Spanish Springs residents. Word of mouth spread faster than we could imagine!

2018 - Expanding Service

Added Lake Sumter Landing and Brownwood to our service area. Our team grew to include our son Mike and daughter Sarah.

2023 - Community Leaders

Now serving all of The Villages with 50+ carts. Named "Best Local Business" by The Villages Daily Sun for three years running.
